Performance Center

The Team App

Next-Gen Telemetry & Team Management for Elite Endurance Racing

Engineered for Victory

The Grid Up Team App is more than just a telemetry tool—it's a comprehensive performance ecosystem. Built by racers for racers, it bridges the gap between raw data and actionable strategy, giving our team the edge needed to dominate the world's most demanding endurance events.

Core Features

Team Hub

Effortlessly manage and switch between your racing teams. A professional entry point that synchronizes your identity across the entire Grid Up ecosystem.

Race Monitor

Stay ahead of the competition with real-time race tracking. Monitor gaps, lap times, and track positions for every car on the grid with glassmorphic clarity.

Data Hub

The ultimate telemetry archive. Reconstruct GPS signatures, analyze throttle/brake traces, and compare historical laps to find every last tenth of a second.

Team Lobby

The social heart of the application. Synchronize with your teammates, see real-time presence, and coordinate your racing efforts before hitting the track.

Fuel Strategy

Precision fuel calculations that adapt to live track conditions. Optimize your pit stops and ensure you finish every race with the perfect fuel margin.

Stint Manager

Track driver swaps and stint lengths with pinpoint accuracy. Know exactly when your next driver needs to be ready and manage rotations effortlessly.

Car Health

Monitor critical vehicle data in real-time. Track engine status, tire wear, and aerodynamic damage to make informed decisions during long stints.

Race Control

The tactical command center. Monitor the entire field, track incident patterns, and coordinate with your driver from a high-impact diagnostic dashboard.

Setup Centre

A centralized hub for car setups. Create, share, and version car setups across the entire team to ensure everyone is running at peak performance.

Dynamic Theming

Make it yours. The built-in theming engine allows for full customization of colors, sidebars, and badges with custom hex code support.

Strategic Use Cases

Endurance Strategy

Coordinate multi-hour stints by tracking fuel consumption and tire wear. Use the Stint Manager to ensure seamless driver transitions under pressure.

Collaborative Setup Dev

Iterate on car setups during practice sessions. Drivers can push setup changes to the Setup Centre for instant feedback and testing by teammates.

Race Management

Spotters and Team Managers use Race Control to monitor the field, track incidents, and provide critical information to the driver in real-time.

Telemetry Sync

Ultra-low latency telemetry bridge for real-time driver feedback.

Dynamic Theming

Fully customizable UI with custom hex code support for team branding.

Secure Identity

Hardware-locked identification via Machine ID for secure team access.
