Event Overview

The 6 Hours of the Glen Powered by VCO is a cornerstone of the IMSA season. This 6-hour endurance race tests multi-class traffic management and speed on one of America's most historic road courses.

Cars Competing

GTP (Cadillac, BMW, Acura, Porsche)
LMP2 (Dallara P217)
GT3 (Mercedes, BMW, Porsche, etc.)

Track Information

Watkins Glen International

The "Boot" layout. Fast, flowing, and unforgiving. With its signature blue armco and high-speed esses, Watkins Glen is a driver favorite.

Race Format

  • Type: Team Event (Multi-class)
  • Duration: 6 Hours
  • License: Class C and above
