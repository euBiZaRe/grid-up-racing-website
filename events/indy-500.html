Event Overview

Known as "The Greatest Spectacle in Racing," the INDY 500 is one of the most prestigious race events in the world. It requires precision, bravery, and a perfect setup to handle the high speeds of the Indianapolis Motor Speedway.

Cars Competing

Dallara IR-18 INDYCAR

Track Information

Indianapolis Motor Speedway

The 2.5-mile oval is a test of nerves. Four distinct turns, all with 9 degrees of banking, require absolute focus for 200 consecutive laps.

Race Format

  • Type: Solo/Team Event
  • Distance: 500 Miles (200 Laps)
  • Qualifying: 4-Lap Average Speed
