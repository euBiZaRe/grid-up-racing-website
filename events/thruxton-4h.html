Event Overview

Thruxton is the fastest circuit in the UK. This 4-hour endurance race for Touring Cars is a showcase of wheel-to-wheel combat and tire management at extremely high speeds.

Thruxton Circuit

A legendary high-speed perimeter circuit. Known for its abrasive surface and high-speed corners like "Church," it's a true test for TCR drivers.
