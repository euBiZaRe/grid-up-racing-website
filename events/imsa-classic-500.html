IMSA Classic 500

April 10-11, 2026

Event Overview

Travel back in time to an era of classic sports car racing with the IMSA Classic 500 at WeatherTech Raceway Laguna Seca. This event celebrates the historic IMSA GT Championship with legendary machinery from the late 70s and early 80s.

Cars Competing

Nissan GTP ZX-T
Audi 90 Quattro GTO

Track Information

WeatherTech Raceway Laguna Seca

A global icon of motorsports, Laguna Seca is famous for the "Corkscrew" and its challenging desert terrain. It provides the perfect backdrop for these high-power classic machines.

Race Format

  • Type: Team Event
  • Duration: 500 Miles / Time Limit
  • License: Class C and above
