Event Overview

The World 600 is NASCAR's longest race. Held over Memorial Day weekend at Charlotte Motor Speedway, it tests a driver's endurance as the track transitions from day to night.

Cars Competing

NASCAR Cup Chevrolet Camaro ZL1
NASCAR Cup Ford Mustang
NASCAR Cup Toyota Camry

Track Information

Charlotte Motor Speedway

A premier 1.5-mile quad-oval. Known for its bumps and high-speed banking, it requires a car that can handle the shifting grip levels as the sun goes down.

Race Format

  • Type: NiS Special Event
  • Distance: 600 Miles (400 Laps)
  • A-Class: License Required
