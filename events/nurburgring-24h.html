Event Overview

The 24 Hours Nürburgring (ADAC 24h Rennen Nürburgring) is the ultimate endurance test. Held on the legendary "Green Hell" combined Nordschleife and GP-Strecke circuit, it features hundreds of cars across dozens of classes.

Cars Competing

GT3 (Porsche, BMW, Ferrari, etc.)
Porsche 992 Cup
GT4 Category
TCR (Audi, Hyundai, Honda)
BMW M2 CS Racing

Track Information

Nürburgring Combined

16.1 miles (25.9 km) of pure adrenaline. With over 170 corners and massive elevation changes, there is no greater challenge in sim racing.

Race Format

  • Type: Team Event (Multi-class)
  • Duration: 24 Hours
  • License: Class C/B and above
